About Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy group formed by JYP Entertainment. They are known for their distinct self-production, their fanbase is called STAY. Stray Kids is known for an experimental, genre-blending sound with heavy influences of hip-hop, EDM, rock, and pop. Their slogan, "You make Stray Kids Stay," reflects the mutual bond between them and their fans.
